Learn More

Hi! We're Becca and Brian! We believe that God's church should be the most welcoming and supportive place on Earth for people with disabilities. Some temporal places are still more inclusive right now, but Christ asked His disciples, "What do ye more than others?" (Matthew 5:47) and we want to have a better answer to that question.

Joseph Smith said "All the minds and spirits that God ever sent into the world are susceptible of enlargement." and Elder Marion D. Hanks continued, "It came to me forcefully that God expects that His handicapped children will be given an opportunity for that enlargement, and that His disciples will accept the great responsibility to be concerned that they are."

If you have a disability, you aren't a placeholder that just need to be stewarded until the next life. You can learn and grow in ways that work for you, and we can support you in strengthening you agency and testimony. We hope these resources can improve people's mindset and understanding of some of the Lord's lost sheep.

Follow Along!

LDS Disabilities Group on FB

  • Clips to Make you Smile (and Think)

    A mix of humor and heart to help change your head. It's ok if supporting people with disabilities doesn't come naturally to you, but that doesn't mean you can't make a difference!

  • Becca's Latest Messages

    Becca and Brian work on all of this together, but Becca prepares some messages all by herself, and we want to make sure those are easily available!

  • Disability Stories

    We can learn a lot from what's happened to others, good and bad. A lot of times people just don't have experience with disabilities, so when someone arrives they don't know what to do. These stories, we hope, can help.

  • Teaching Resources

    A combination of general strategies and weekly Come Folloe Me suggestions to help make your lessons more inclusive.

  • Surveys

    Disability experiences can vary quite a bit by region and even by ward. We are collecting feedback on what is and isn't working so we can all learn together how best to support people with disabilities.

  • Ordinances

    Church ordinances can be tricky to navigate when someone's independence is in question. We share a bit about Becca's experiences and ideas for how to proceed in your own ward.

  • About Us

    A little more about Brian and Becca, how we got here, and what we're hoping to accomplish with this project.
